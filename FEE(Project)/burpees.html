Burpees Tutorial

How to Do a Proper Burpee

  1. Start standing: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides.
  2. Squat down: Bend your knees and place your hands on the floor in front of you, shoulder-width apart.
  3. Kick back: Jump your feet back to a plank position, keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  4. Perform a push-up: Lower your chest to the floor, then press back up to the plank position.
  5. Jump feet forward: Jump your feet back towards your hands, landing in a squat position.
  6. Jump up: Explosively jump up, reaching your arms overhead. Land softly and immediately go into the next rep.

Benefits of Burpees

  • Full-body workout that engages multiple muscle groups
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness and endurance
  • Builds strength in the legs, core, chest, and arms
  • Burns a significant amount of calories
  • Enhances agility and coordination

Tips for Perfecting Your Burpee

  • Maintain a steady pace and control your movements throughout the exercise.
  • Keep your core engaged to protect your lower back.
  • Land softly when jumping to reduce impact on your joints.
  • For beginners, start with modified versions, such as step-back burpees or no push-up burpees.
  • Focus on form and technique to maximize effectiveness and prevent injuries.
