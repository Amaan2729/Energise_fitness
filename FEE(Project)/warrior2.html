Warrior 2 Tutorial

How to Perform Warrior II

  1. Start in Mountain Pose: Stand tall with feet hip-width apart and arms at your sides.
  2. Step back: Take a wide step back with your left foot, turning your left toes slightly inwards, and bend your right knee to 90 degrees.
  3. Stretch your arms: Extend your arms parallel to the ground, reaching through your fingertips. Keep your shoulders relaxed.
  4. Gaze over your right hand: Look past your right fingertips and hold the pose for several breaths.
  5. Repeat: Switch sides to balance the pose, performing the same movements on your left side.

Benefits of Warrior II

  • Strengthens the legs, hips, and shoulders
  • Improves balance and stability
  • Increases stamina and concentration
  • Opens the chest and hips
  • Boosts mental focus and endurance

Tips for Perfecting Warrior II

  • Keep your front knee directly over your ankle for proper alignment.
  • Press evenly through both feet to ground yourself in the pose.
  • Engage your core to maintain balance and stability.
  • Relax your shoulders and avoid tensing your neck.
  • Hold the pose for several breaths, then switch sides.
