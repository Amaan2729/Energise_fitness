Tree Pose Tutorial

How to Perform Tree Pose

  1. Start in Mountain Pose: Stand tall with your feet together, arms at your sides, and weight evenly distributed on both feet.
  2. Shift your weight: Slowly shift your weight onto one foot (e.g., left foot), keeping the leg straight and strong.
  3. Position your other foot: Bring the sole of your right foot to your inner left thigh or calf (avoid placing it on the knee).
  4. Bring hands to prayer: Once balanced, bring your hands together in front of your chest in a prayer position.
  5. Hold and focus: Hold the pose, focusing on a point in front of you to maintain balance. Switch sides and repeat.

Benefits of Tree Pose

  • Improves balance and stability in the legs
  • Strengthens the core and lower body
  • Increases focus and concentration
  • Stretches the thighs, groin, and shoulders
  • Helps build self-awareness and mindfulness

Tips for Perfecting Tree Pose

  • Engage your core to maintain stability.
  • Press the sole of your raised foot firmly against your standing leg.
  • Keep your hips square and avoid leaning to one side.
  • Focus on a steady point ahead to help with balance.
  • Start with your foot lower on your leg if you're new, then progress higher as you improve.
