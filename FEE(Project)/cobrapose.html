Cobra Pose Tutorial

How to Perform Cobra Pose

  1. Start on your belly: Lie face down with your legs extended and the tops of your feet pressing into the mat.
  2. Place your hands under your shoulders: Position your palms on the floor next to your chest, elbows close to your body.
  3. Lift your chest: Inhale and press into your hands to gently lift your chest, keeping your pelvis and legs grounded.
  4. Draw shoulders back: Pull your shoulder blades together and down your back, opening up the chest.
  5. Hold and breathe: Hold the pose for a few breaths, then slowly lower yourself back down.

Benefits of Cobra Pose

  • Strengthens the spine and lower back
  • Improves posture and flexibility
  • Stretches the chest, shoulders, and abdomen
  • Relieves tension in the upper body
  • Increases energy and alleviates stress

Tips for Perfecting Cobra Pose

  • Engage your core to support your lower back.
  • Avoid overextending your neck by gazing forward or slightly down.
  • Keep your elbows slightly bent to avoid locking them.
  • Press evenly into both hands and keep your shoulders relaxed.
  • Hold the pose for a few breaths, gradually increasing the duration as you build strength.
