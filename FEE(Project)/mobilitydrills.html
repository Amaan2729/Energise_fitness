Mobility Drills

How to Perform Mobility Drills

  1. Warm-Up: Start with light aerobic activity to increase blood flow and prepare your muscles.
  2. Dynamic Stretching: Include movements like arm circles, leg swings, and torso twists to loosen joints and muscles.
  3. Joint Rotations: Rotate your major joints (ankles, knees, hips, shoulders, wrists) to enhance mobility.
  4. Hip Openers: Incorporate exercises like hip circles and butterfly stretches to improve hip flexibility.
  5. Cool Down: Finish with static stretches to help maintain flexibility and relax your muscles.

Benefits of Mobility Drills

  • Increases range of motion in joints
  • Reduces the risk of injuries
  • Improves overall athletic performance
  • Enhances posture and alignment
  • Promotes better recovery after workouts
