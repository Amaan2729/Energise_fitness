Squat Tutorial

How to Do a Proper Squat

  1. Stand tall: Position your feet shoulder-width apart with your toes slightly pointed out. Keep your chest up and your back straight.
  2. Lower your body: Bend your knees and push your hips back as if you're sitting in a chair. Keep your weight on your heels and go as low as you can while maintaining proper form.
  3. Return to standing: Press through your heels to return to the starting position, fully extending your legs and squeezing your glutes at the top.

Benefits of Squats

  • Strengthens the lower body muscles including quads, hamstrings, and glutes
  • Improves core strength and stability
  • Enhances flexibility and balance
  • Increases functional strength for daily activities

Tips for Perfecting Your Squat

  • Keep your chest up and avoid rounding your back.
  • Ensure your knees track over your toes, not caving inward.
  • Engage your core throughout the movement.
  • Use a mirror to check your form if possible.
