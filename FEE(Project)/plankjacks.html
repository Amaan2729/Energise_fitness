Plank Jacks Tutorial

How to Perform Plank Jacks

  1. Get into plank position: Begin in a high plank position with your hands directly under your shoulders, body in a straight line, and core engaged.
  2. Jump your feet out and in: Keeping your upper body stable, jump your feet out wider than hip-width apart, then quickly jump them back together.
  3. Maintain control: Focus on keeping your hips level and your core tight to prevent your lower back from sagging.
  4. Breathe steadily: Breathe out as you jump your feet out and breathe in as you bring them back together.
  5. Repeat: Perform the exercise for a set duration, maintaining speed and form throughout.

Benefits of Plank Jacks

  • Improves cardiovascular fitness and burns calories
  • Strengthens the core, shoulders, and legs
  • Enhances stability and coordination
  • Incorporates both cardio and strength in one exercise
  • Ideal for high-intensity interval training (HIIT) routines

Tips for Perfecting Your Plank Jacks

  • Keep your core engaged to stabilize your body and protect your lower back.
  • Ensure your hands remain directly under your shoulders for proper support.
  • Jump your feet with controlled movements to prevent bouncing or arching your back.
  • Start slow to master the form before increasing speed.
  • Incorporate this exercise into your HIIT workouts for maximum fat burn and conditioning.
